About

Xinyan Lu (卢鑫炎) is a Ph.D candidate at College of Computer Science, Zhejiang University, as a student of Prof. Yueting Zhuang. He joined the DCD laboratory in 2010 after he received his B.S. degree at School of Mathematics & Computational Science, Sun Yat-sen University.

His research insterests are cross-media retrieval and learning to rank.
