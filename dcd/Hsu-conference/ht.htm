Transportation

How to get to Conference Venue, at Zhejiang University, Yuquan Campus?

  1. From Hangzhou Xiao-Shan Airport, take an airport shuttle to Wulin Gate, and then either take a taxi or Bus line #28 (or 21) to Zhejiang University, Yuquan Campus.
  2. From Hangzhou Train Station, take a taxi or Bus line #21 to Zhejiang University, Yuquan Campus.
  3. From Hangzhou Long-distance Bus Station, take a taxi to Zhejiang University, Yuquan Campus.
  4. From Shanghai Hongqiao Airport, take an airport shuttle to Wulin Gate, then either take a taxi or Bus line #28 (or 21) to Zhejiang University, Yuquan Campus.
  5. From Shanghai Pudong Airport to Dragon Sports Center (which is near our campus):
    10:30 12:00 13:30 15:30 17:30 19:00
    From Dragon Sports Center to Shanghai Pudong Airport:
    7:00 8:30 10:30 12:30 14:30 16:00
  6. From Beijing or any other cities, take a flight from your city to Hangzhou Xiao-Shan Airport, and then take an airport shuttle to Wulin Gate. At Wulin Gate, either take a taxi or Bus line #28 (or 21) to Zhejiang University, Yuquan Campus.
  7. You can also take trains from Shanghai to Hangzhou on both Shanghai station and Shanghai south station. However, Shanghai south station has more trains to Hangzhou. There are many of trains from Shanghai to Hangzhou every day.
